Executive Summary

Investment Thesis

Partnership with INTERSPORT CZ represents a strategic opportunity to expand ENERVIT's retail presence in the Czech market. With 38 stores nationwide and annual revenue of ~900M CZK, INTERSPORT provides immediate access to the active sports consumer segment. Our analysis projects first-year revenue potential of 2.5-5.5M CZK with attractive margins for both parties.

Margin Summary

The EST35 pricing structure delivers healthy margins for both VITAR Sport (~55% gross) and INTERSPORT (~42% gross). This pricing is sustainable and competitive with other premium sports nutrition brands in the market.

Risk Analysis

Competition Response

Risk: Established competitors may respond with aggressive pricing or promotions

Mitigation: Focus on ENERVIT's unique C2:1 technology and premium positioning; avoid price wars

Slow Adoption

Risk: Store staff unfamiliarity with products may limit active selling

Mitigation: Comprehensive training program; incentivize store staff with samples and knowledge

Inventory Management

Risk: Product expiration due to slow rotation in some stores

Mitigation: Expiry exchange policy; monitor sell-through rates; adjust assortment per store

Supply Chain

Risk: Stock-outs during peak demand periods

Mitigation: Maintain safety stock; forecast seasonal peaks; utilize existing VITAR warehouse infrastructure

Recommendations

Strategic Recommendation

Proceed with INTERSPORT partnership using phased rollout approach. The opportunity presents attractive unit economics, strategic market positioning, and manageable risk profile.

Implementation Roadmap

Phase Scope KPIs
Phase 1: Pilot 5-8 stores in Prague/major cities 50+ units/store/month; positive staff feedback
Phase 2: Expansion Rollout to 30 stores 80+ units/store/month; 90% availability
Phase 3: Full Coverage All 38+ stores 100+ units/store/month; category leadership

Key Success Factors

  • Staff Training: Invest in comprehensive product education for store employees
  • POS Excellence: Deploy high-quality merchandising materials in prominent locations
  • Inventory Monitoring: Weekly sell-through tracking; proactive replenishment
  • Marketing Alignment: Coordinate in-store promotions with ENERVIT brand campaigns
  • Relationship Management: Regular check-ins with INTERSPORT buyers and store managers
